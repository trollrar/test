Working at Sandbox Interactive

Albion Online launched in July 2017 with over 250,000 Founders. In May 2018, the game launched on Steam, expanding its player base even further. As the game continues to grow and develop, we are constantly working on new features, content, and projects in both our production and operations departments.

We are looking for great people to join our team and help us make the world of Albion even more enjoyable and widely known.

If you are an expert in your field, passionate about games, and eager to work on this exciting product, then you might be just the right person for us!

We are always looking for great people to add to our team, so don't hesitate to send your application documents (including CV and cover letter) to jobs@sandbox-interactive.com.

About Sandbox Interactive

Sandbox Interactive is an ambitious game development studio based in the heart of Berlin. The company was founded in 2012 and currently employs a team of 30, bringing together some of the best in the industry. We offer a professional work environment with flat hierarchies and a communicative, efficient team. Our work culture is all about transparency and communication, and offering our employees a high level of responsibility in goal-oriented tasks. Join us and be part of our team!

About Albion Online

Albion Online is the first true cross-platform MMO, and is playable on Windows, Mac, Linux, iOS and Android. The game offers players an extensive character customization system with no class restrictions, a completely player-driven economy, and thrilling PvP battles. The action of the open game world revolves around besieging, claiming and defending territories. Albion Online launched on July 17, 2017, and is currently played by thousands of players from all over the world.
